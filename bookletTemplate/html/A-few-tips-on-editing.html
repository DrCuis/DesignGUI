1.2 A few tips on editing ¶

A subsection divides a section in smaller parts.

Pictures must be copied in each Chapter-XX/img folder and be prefixed with the chapter number to avoid collision on html output.

Then to insert an image, use the @figure macro with three arguments: caption, filename without extension and width expressed in centimeter: @figure{Cuis, ch00-img1, 8}.
